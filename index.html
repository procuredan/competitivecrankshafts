How It Works

Straightforward, mail-in friendly, and built around getting your crank back right.

①

Send It In

Reach out for a quote and shipping details. Pull your crank (or send the whole bottom end) and get it to the shop.

②

Teardown & Rebuild

We disassemble, inspect, replace worn rods, bearings and seals, weld where needed, then press, true, and balance the assembly.

③

Verified & Returned

Every crank is measured for runout and checked before it ships back to you, ready to drop in and run.

Sending a crank in?

Include your contact info and engine details with your core so we can turn it around fast. Have questions before you ship? Get in touch and we'll walk you through it.

About Competitive Crankshafts

Competitive Crankshafts is a specialist repair shop in Garden City, Idaho, focused on one thing and doing it well: rebuilding 2-stroke crankshafts. No guesswork, no batch-and-bill — just careful, hands-on work on every crank that comes through the door.

Two-stroke cranks live a hard life. We bring them back to spec with quality components, proper welding where it's called for, and tight truing tolerances so your engine runs smooth and lasts. Proudly built right here in the USA.
