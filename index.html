Common Questions

A few quick answers — see the full FAQ for more.

What does a crankshaft rebuild include?
A full teardown, inspection, and reassembly with new connecting rods, big-end bearings and seals as needed, plus welding where required, then pressing, truing to tight runout, and balancing.
Do you offer mail-in service?
Yes — we accept crankshafts and complete bottom ends from anywhere in the country. See how to send your core.
How much does a rebuild cost?
Pricing is flat-rate by make and model. See the full price list for watercraft, snowmobile, and motorcycle/ATV.
