Motorcycle & ATV Crankshaft Rebuilding

Dirt bikes and ATVs live at the rev limiter, and a tired crank shows up as vibration, lost power, and bottom-end noise. We rebuild motorcycle and ATV crankshafts — 2-stroke and 4-stroke singles, plus the Yamaha Banshee twin.

We service Yamaha, Honda, Suzuki, Kawasaki and KTM singles, and the Banshee can be built standard or with welded webs for hard use. Quality connecting rods and bearings, pressed, trued and balanced.

Whether it's a play bike, a race engine, or a Banshee build, a straight, balanced crank is the foundation of reliable power.
