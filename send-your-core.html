Send Your Core — Mail-In Rebuild Service

Rebuilding your 2-stroke crank is easy from anywhere in the country. Here's how.

①

Get a quote

Contact us with your make, model, year and what the crank needs. We'll confirm pricing and give you shipping details.

②

Remove the crankshaft

Pull the crank from your engine, or send the complete bottom end if you'd rather we split the cases. Either works.

③

Clean and tag it

Wipe off heavy grease and oil. Include a note with your name, phone, email, and engine details so your core never gets mixed up.

④

Package it securely

Wrap the crank well and box it so the journals and rod ends can't bang around in transit. Use a sturdy box and plenty of padding.

⑤

Ship it to us

Send it to Competitive Crankshafts, 507 E 45th Street, Unit 300, Garden City, ID 83714. We recommend insuring the package.

⑥

We rebuild & return

We rebuild, weld, true and balance your crank, verify it, and ship it back ready to run.
